Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (80% percent beef and 20% fat content for a juicier lasagna)
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Red Wine (or Beef Broth)
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Dried Thyme
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Egg

How to Make Lasagna

  1. Brown the Beef

    2. Add oil to a deep pan and sautee onion and beef until browned
    5 minutes then add garlic and stir for another minute.

  2. Finish the Sauce

    3. Pour in 1/4 cup of wineand stir until almost evaporated.
    Add marinara, salt, pepper, thyme, sugar and parsley; bring to a simmer, then cover and cook for 5 minutes.

  3. Make the Cheese Filling

    4. Combine all the cheese sauce ingredients and 1 cup of mozzarella in a mixing bowl (reserve the rest for later).

  4. Prep

    5. Preheat your oven to 375ºF and cook the lasagna noodles in a pot of well-salted water until al dente.

  5. Assemble

    6. Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
    Add 3 noodles, followed by 1/3 of the meat sauce, 1 cup of mozzarella, and half of the ricotta mixture.
    Repeat, then for the top layer, add 3 noodles, 1/3 of the meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella.

  6. Bake

    7. Cover with foil and bake at 375˚F for 45 minutes, then remove the foil and broil for 3 to 5 minutes, or until the cheese is lightly browned.

  7. Rest

    8. Let the lasagna rest for about 30 minutes before cutting and serving.
